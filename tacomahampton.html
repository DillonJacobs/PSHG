HAMPTON INN AND SUITES
TACOMA

Stay in the Hampton Inn & Suites Tacoma-Mall hotel, enveloped by everything we love - including a grand view of Mt. Rainier. Treat yourself to an upscale shopping and dining experience at Tacoma Mallor Lakewood Towne Center. Enjoy a concert at the Tacoma Dome, experience unique attractions like Northwest Trek Wild Life Park, the Museum of Glass and the LeMay Car Museum or take the children to experience the zoo and aquarium at Point Defiance Park. Play a few relaxed games of golf at Chamber's Bay Golf Club.

Visiting for business? We're centrally located near Boeing, General Dynamics, Intel, and Joint Base Lewis McChord and other major companies. Corporate guests can stay on top of deadlines at the business center, complete with photocopying, printing and express mail facilities and host events in the spacious, modern meeting spaces with support staff on-hand to assist.
