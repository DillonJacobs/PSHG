Whether you're visiting Seattle for a weekend trip or an extended stay, the Residence Inn Seattle University District can act as a contemporary home base while you explore the exciting University District. Located just steps from the campus of the University of Washington, our hotel welcomes guests with spacious, well-appointed hotel suites, each offering separate areas for living and sleeping, a fully equipped kitchen, free Wi-Fi, and a flat-screen TV. In addition, our hotel provides helpful amenities to help you maximize your stay in Seattle, Washington, including complimentary full hot breakfast daily, a fitness center, a heated indoor pool, and a friendly, attentive staff. Convenient for the extended-stay traveler, our hotel features a free grocery shopping service, plenty of space to work and relax, and a prime location in the University District, moments from the University of Washington; we're also close to Seattle University. A refreshing hotel stay awaits you at the Residence Inn Seattle University District.
