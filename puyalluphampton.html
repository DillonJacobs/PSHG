HAMPTON INN & SUITES
PUYALLUP

The Hampton Inn & Suites Tacoma/Puyallup hotel is located in the heart of Puyallup, Washington, and nestled at the foot of Mount Rainer. This Puyallup hotel is conveniently located next door to Multicare Good Samaritan Hospital, steps from the Puyallup Fair & Event Center and a short drive to Puyallup's South Hill Mall. Enjoy the ideal location—just minutes from Tacoma, with easy access to Joint Base Lewis-McCord, the James Hardie Building and the Boeing Fredrickson Plant.

Stay in a modern guest room at this Puyallup hotel or upgrade to a spacious suite. Enjoy a restful night on our clean and fresh Hampton® be, and stay connected with free WiFi offered in every room. Begin each say with Hampton’s free hot breakfast and sample our world-famous waffles, or just grab one of our Hampton On the Run® Breakfast Bags on any weekday morning for a delicious breakfast to take away.

Work out in the hotel’s fitness center with cardio machines, or swim laps in the indoor pool. Stay on top of work in this Puyallup hotel’s fully equipped business center with printing and photocopying access, or host a meeting in the 1,246 sq. ft. meeting room for up to 80 guests.
